What I Do

Research

My academic journey has spanned a wide array of compelling research topics across various disciplines. I have shown how smartphones can detect atmospheric tides and examined the role of viscoelastic properties in shaping the deformation and orbits of planets and moons. Additionally, I applied machine learning techniques to laboratory experiments on geological materials to improve models and deepen our understanding of Earth's dynamic processes. This drive for meaningful research has further propelled me into the realm of finance and sustainable investing. Here, I apply my expertise in data science and Earth science to evaluate financial decisions with a focus on sustainability

Numerical & Data-Driven Modeling

This is where my training in physics and mathematics meets my programming skills. Modeling is where I can test and quantify my research ideas and make predictions. I love seeing how equations come alive in a model, and how many valuable insights we can gain when integrating real-world data. In both my PhD and Master's research projects, I navigated the confluence of data science and physics to solve scientific puzzles, the outcomes of which were published in peer-reviewed academic journals

Teaching & Outreach

Communicating scientific concepts is a great passion of mine. Some topics in Earth sciences require a physics understanding, which can be intimidating for people with no relevant background. However, I believe that every topic can be broken down into simple ingredients, and with effective illustrations and demonstrations, learning even advanced topics can become an exciting adventure. My teaching experience ranges from high school to graduate-level classes, and it consistently thrills me to witness students growing more curious about nature as they acquire deeper knowledge

Playing Guitar and Writing Music

My non-analytical creative outlet. Music is a great source of energy for me, and guitar playing is my favorite hobby. Over the years, I've had the fortune of playing with multiple bands, writing songs, and translating emotions into soulful guitar melodies
